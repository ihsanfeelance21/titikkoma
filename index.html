ABOUT

Founded in April 2017, Titik Koma comes form an idea that everyone can enjoy Coffee. it's all started at a small rented building on Jalan Dharmawangsa. By 2019, Titik Koma has 5 branches, one of them located in Bandung

Language of specialty coffee culture is a great coffee taste and it rests on the basis of responsible & respectful conduct "from-seed-to-cup".

Our love for specialty coffee is never-ending. We spend hundreds of days a year at origin working with the best growers on the planet and only the best beans make the grade.
